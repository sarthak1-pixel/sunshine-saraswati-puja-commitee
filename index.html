Our Mission

The Sunshine Enclave Saraswati Puja Committee, established in **2011**, is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage associated with Maa Saraswati, the Goddess of knowledge and arts. Our annual festival is more than a religious ritual; it is a **community cornerstone**, fostering unity, creativity, and learning among residents of all ages.

We strive to organize an event that is spiritually enriching, culturally vibrant, and socially inclusive. By blending traditional *puja* rites with modern community engagement, we ensure the spirit of wisdom and harmony flourishes in our Enclave.
